The Challenge

Logistics powers economies—
but much of its intelligence is still hidden

Every day, thousands of shipments travel between ports, warehouses, factories, distribution centres, and retail locations across Africa. These movements generate valuable operational information.

Yet that information is often fragmented across spreadsheets, messaging apps, paper documents, and disconnected software systems.

The challenge is not a lack of data. The challenge is turning data into intelligence that organisations can trust and act on.

  • Delays are discovered after they happen
  • Financial institutions assess businesses with limited operational visibility
  • Insurance decisions rely on incomplete risk information
  • Customers lack real-time transparency into their shipments
  • Governments have no unified view of national logistics performance
Traditional logistics software tells you where shipments are.

Via Bahati explains what is happening, predicts what is likely to happen next, recommends the best course of action, and continuously learns from every operational outcome.

This transforms logistics from reactive operations into intelligent decision-making.

Company
Via Bahati exists to build the intelligence infrastructure that African logistics has always needed but never had.

Founded in Accra, Ghana, by Bright Fedy Kulafe — Executive Director of Vestige Logistics Gh Ltd — Via Bahati was built from direct experience inside Ghana's freight network. We saw the data. We felt the gaps. We built the layer that was missing.

Our belief is that African logistics deserves the same quality of operational intelligence available to global supply chains — and that this intelligence should serve every part of the ecosystem: operators, financiers, insurers, and governments alike.

Our Mission

To transform fragmented logistics data into trusted operational intelligence that helps African businesses, institutions, and governments make faster and better decisions.

Our Vision

A connected logistics intelligence network spanning West Africa — where every shipment contributes to a shared understanding of how goods move across the continent.

Our Values

Intelligence over assumption. Evidence over opinion. Infrastructure over product. We build for the long term, for every stakeholder, with trust at the centre.

Security & Trust

Built for organisations where reliability is essential

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Complete data isolation between organisations. Each tenant's data is segregated at the database level with row-level security policies enforced on every table.

Encrypted Data

All data encrypted in transit and at rest. API keys stored as SHA-256 hashes. Portal passwords hashed with PBKDF2 at 100,000 iterations.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions across all platform features. Users see only the data and functions relevant to their role.

Continuous Monitoring

Real-time operational monitoring, comprehensive audit logs, automatic backups, and webhook validation on all external integrations.
